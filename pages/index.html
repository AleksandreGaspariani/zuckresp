ZhukConsult is a small privately-owned consulting company, founded in 2017 in Darmstadt, Germany.

As of January 2020, we are based in Germany’s historical city of Aachen, also known by its French and traditional English name of Aix-la-Chapelle. The city has been the royal residence of the emperor Charlemagne or Charles the Great (Latin: Carolus Magnus, German: Karl der Große) and later, it served as the principal coronation site of Holy Roman emperors and German kings from the Middle Ages to the Reformation.

Our team is proud to provide international and multilingual services from this unique Germany’s westernmost major city, where three nations and three languages converge.
